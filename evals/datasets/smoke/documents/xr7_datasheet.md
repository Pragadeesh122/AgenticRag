# XR-7 Industrial Controller — Product Datasheet

## Overview
The XR-7 is a ruggedized industrial controller designed for factory automation environments. It supports PLC programming via IEC 61131-3 and integrates with standard SCADA systems.

## Specifications
- **Processor:** ARM Cortex-A72, 1.8 GHz quad-core
- **Memory:** 4 GB DDR4 RAM
- **Storage:** 64 GB eMMC + expandable via SD card
- **I/O Ports:** 16 digital inputs, 8 digital outputs, 4 analog inputs (0-10V)
- **Communication:** Ethernet (2x GigE), RS-485, CAN bus, Wi-Fi 6
- **Power:** 24V DC nominal (18-32V range), 15W typical
- **Operating Temperature:** -20C to 60C
- **Ingress Protection:** IP65

## Warranty
The XR-7 comes with a standard warranty period of 24 months from the date of purchase. Extended warranty options of 36 months and 48 months are available through authorized distributors. The warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not cover damage from improper installation or environmental conditions outside the rated specifications.
