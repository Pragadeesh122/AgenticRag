# Q1 2025 Quarterly Business Report

## Revenue Summary

Total revenue for Q1 2025 was $4.2 million, representing a 15% increase over Q1 2024 ($3.65 million). Key contributors:

- **Product Sales:** $3.1M (up 18% YoY)
- **Service Contracts:** $0.8M (up 8% YoY)
- **Training & Consulting:** $0.3M (up 12% YoY)

## Regional Breakdown

| Region | Revenue | Growth |
|--------|---------|--------|
| North America | $2.1M | +20% |
| Europe | $1.2M | +10% |
| Asia-Pacific | $0.9M | +12% |

## Top Customers

1. Meridian Manufacturing — $620K (15% of revenue)
2. Atlas Industrial — $480K (11% of revenue)
3. Pacific Automation — $350K (8% of revenue)

## Gross Margin

Overall gross margin improved to 42%, up from 38% in Q1 2024, driven by supply chain optimizations and higher-margin service contract growth.

## Headcount

Total headcount at end of Q1: 87 employees (up from 78 at end of Q4 2024).
- Engineering: 34
- Sales & Marketing: 22
- Operations: 18
- General & Administrative: 13

## Outlook

Q2 pipeline is strong at $5.8M weighted, with several large enterprise deals expected to close. The XR-7 product launch is driving significant new customer interest.
