# Factory Safety Guidelines — Rev 3.2

## Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

All personnel entering the production floor must wear:
1. Safety glasses with side shields (ANSI Z87.1 rated)
2. Steel-toed boots (ASTM F2413 compliant)
3. High-visibility vest (Class 2 minimum)
4. Hearing protection in zones marked above 85 dB

## Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) Procedures

Before servicing any automated equipment:
1. Notify the shift supervisor and affected operators
2. Shut down the equipment using the normal stopping procedure
3. Isolate all energy sources (electrical, pneumatic, hydraulic)
4. Apply individual locks and tags at each isolation point
5. Verify zero-energy state by attempting to restart
6. Perform the required maintenance
7. Remove locks and tags only after verifying all tools and personnel are clear

Violation of LOTO procedures is grounds for immediate disciplinary action up to and including termination.

## Emergency Contacts
- Plant Safety Officer: ext. 4100
- Emergency Response Team: ext. 9111
- Environmental Compliance: ext. 4200

## Incident Reporting

All safety incidents, including near-misses, must be reported within 4 hours using Form SF-200. The safety committee reviews all reports within 48 hours.
